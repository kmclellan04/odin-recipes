Peanut Butter and Jam Sandwich

Description

This peanut butter and jam sandwich (pb&j) is an all time classic and is super easy to prepare!

"Nothing fancy, just a classic. Either smooth or crunch peanut butter is acceptable. Classically, the jelly is either strawberry or grape."

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons strawberry jam

Directions

  1. Spread the peanut butter on 1 slice of bread
  2. Spread the jelly on the other slice
  3. Put the 2 pieces of bread together to form a sandwich
  4. Toddler adaptation: Cut off crusts of the bread
  5. Enjoy!
